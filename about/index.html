This site is written (content), written (code), and produced by Lloyd Thompson.

Lloyd Thompson is currently a user experience specialist a at large American computer and mobile device manufacturer, as well as halfway through a Master of Science in Human Factors in Information Design from Bentley University.

Lloyd currently resides in Austin, TX, where after 11 years he's finally become accustomed to the heat.

If everything goes according to plan, this site will serve as a posting point for a variety of topics - including food, coffee, design, development, fashion and shoes.

Technological Manifest

The following instruments, languages and software objects were used in the creation of this site over three weeks in July 2013:

  • HTML5 compiled into static pages using Jeckyl and Markdown
  • CSS3 compiled via LESS & jeckyl-less
  • JavaScript with jquery
  • Twitter Bootstrap, because I'm a little bit lazy and don't want to write my own "pull-right" class.
  • Typekit by Adobe, providing Freight Sans Pro and Adobe Caslon Pro
  • Font Awesome, an icon-based font for the modern web. Never use your "caret-up.gif" or "caret-down.gif" again...
  • Photos taken with a Nikon D600
  • Coffee brewed with a Rancilio Miss Silvia Espresso Machine, brewing beans purchased from Houndstooth Coffee in Austin, TX.
